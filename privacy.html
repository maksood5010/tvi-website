Hillian Android App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26-sep-2023

Introduction

Welcome to Hillian, the Android app designed to help nurses efficiently manage patient care at our hospital. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Hillian app (referred to as "the App"). Your privacy and the security of your personal information are of utmost importance to us. By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • a. Information You Provide

    Account Information: When you create an account within the App, we may collect your name, contact information, employee ID, and other necessary credentials for identification and authentication.

    Patient Information: As a nurse, you may input and access patient information, including names, medical records, diagnoses, treatment plans, and other healthcare-related data. This information is used solely for patient care and management within the hospital.

Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Device Information:

    We may collect information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers, to optimize the App's performance.

  • Log Data:

    We automatically collect log data, including IP addresses, browser type, and timestamps of your interactions with the App, for diagnostic and analytical purposes.

  • Usage Analytics:

    We may collect information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use, the patients you manage, and the duration of your sessions. This data helps us improve our services and better tailor the App to your needs.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality to assist nurses in patient care management.
  • To personalize your experience within the App and tailor it to your role and responsibilities.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries, providing support, and sending important updates or notifications related to patient care.
  • To analyze how the App is used, diagnose technical issues, and conduct research to enhance our healthcare services.
  • To protect the security and integrity of patient data and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations and standards.
